Who We Are

MB Superb Logistics is a premier logistics broker focusing on vehicle transportation. We help you relocate your vehicle, finding the best carriers to fit your needs. We are licensed and insured. Our website address is: https://mbsuperblogistics.com.

What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from customers in a variety of ways, but not limited to, when customers visit our site, fill out any forms provided to our customers or found on our site, and in connection with any other activities, services, features, or resources we make available on our site. Customers may be asked for, as appropriate, name, email address, vehicle information, pick-up address, delivery address, phone number, payment information, and other identifying information related to their transport.

We may collect information on other individuals from customers if customers elect to have another individual involved in the pick-up or delivery process. We will collect personal identification information from and about customers, including:

Information provided by customers, such as when they voluntarily submit such information to us;

Information collected automatically as customers navigate through our Site, which may include domain address, IP addresses, internet browser, and information collected through cookies.

Customers can always refuse to supply us their personal identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain Site-related activities or prevent our ability to provide customers our services.

SMS/MMS Messaging

Customers Opt In to receive text messages from our agents related to your inquiry. This agreement is done via a checkbox in the quote request form on our site or by responding to an automated text message during or post calls with our agents. Customers can reply STOP to Opt Out or HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Frequency of messages may vary. We do not sell or share any SMS consent or personal information to third parties or affiliates for SMS marketing purposes.

Web Browser Cookies

Our Site may use first-party and third-party “cookies” to enhance Customer experience, such as for internal analytics purposes and how we can improve our Site and services. A customer’s web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. Customers may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If a customer chooses to refuse cookies, please note that some parts of the Site may not function properly as a result.
