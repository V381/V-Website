May 15, 2025 The MB Superb Team Company Profile Auto Transport

Welcome to MB Superb Logistics — Your Trusted Auto Transport Partner

At MB Superb Logistics, we understand that shipping your vehicle is more than just moving a car, truck, or SUV — it's about trust, transparency, and peace of mind. MB Superb team brings nearly decade of industry experience, ensuring that your vehicle is in expert hands from pickup to delivery.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced and Reliable

Each member of our team has nearly ten years of hands-on experience in auto transportation. This wealth of knowledge allows us to handle your vehicle with care and professionalism, regardless of the distance or vehicle type. We offer direct customer service, transparent pricing with no changes once an agreement is signed, and are always available to serve you.

Comprehensive Vehicle Transport Services

We transport cars, trucks, and SUVs across the country, providing tailored solutions to meet your specific needs. Whether you're relocating, buying or selling a vehicle, or need fleet transport, we've got you covered.

Honest and Transparent Pricing

Unlike many brokers in the industry, we believe in honesty. We provide clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. We won't promise you a price only to surprise you with extra charges upon delivery. For example, some companies add hidden fees when transporting vehicles across regions with fewer loads. Our reputation matters — what you see is what you pay.

Strict Carrier Selection

We only work with carriers that have at least three years of experience and are fully insured. Many brokers cut costs by using uninsured or unlicensed carriers, risking your vehicle's safety. Our trusted carriers have long-standing relationships with us and maintain the highest standards of safety and insurance coverage.

Direct Communication & Real-Time Updates

We believe in keeping you informed. That's why we share driver contact details so you can receive real-time updates during your vehicle's journey. Transportation and communication are key to a smooth experience.

Safety & Insurance

While we strive for 100% safe transport, accidents can happen. That's why every carrier we work with holds a $1,000,000 insurance policy. In the unlikely event of damage or total loss, we will guide you through the process of submitting a claim. Choosing a company like ours ensures your vehicle is protected by proper insurance — a crucial reason why we don't cut corners on costs.

Why We Stand Out

Unlike large, impersonal corporations that often avoid responsibility when issues arise, we prioritize direct contact and personalized service. We're small, dedicated, and committed to making your experience as smooth and transparent as possible. When you choose MB Superb Logistics, you're not just another shipment — you're a valued customer.

Thank You for Choosing MB Superb Logistics

We hope you'll give us the opportunity to serve you. Our team is here to ensure your vehicle is transported safely, efficiently, and with complete transparency.

Thank you for trusting MB Superb Logistics — where experience meets integrity.
