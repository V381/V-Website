### **1. Is my vehicle insured during transport?**

Yes. All carriers we assign are fully insured and FMCSA approved.

### **2. When do I pay?**

A small deposit is paid to MB Superb Logistics.

The remaining balance is paid **upon delivery, directly to the carrier**.

### **3. How long does shipping take?**

* Short routes (200-500 miles): 1-3 days\
  Medium routes (500-1500 miles): 3-5 days
* Long routes (1500+ miles): 3 to 7 days

**4. Can I put luggage in the vehicle?**

Up to **100–125 lbs** is allowed in the trunk or back seat.

### **5. Do I need to be present at pickup or delivery?**

Yes, or you can assign a trusted representative.

### **6. What if my car doesn’t run?**

We can transport non-running vehicles with a special winch-equipped carrier.
