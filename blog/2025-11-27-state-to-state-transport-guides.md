# **NY → FL Auto Transport Guide**

Shipping your vehicle from New York to Florida is one of the busiest lanes in the U.S.

### **Average Transport Time:**

3–5 days

### **Most Common Route:**

I-95 corridor

New York → New Jersey → Carolinas → Georgia → Florida

### **Average Pricing:**


Depends on vehicle & season, but typically:





**Small cars $900 to $1200**

**SUV/s / Trucks: $1000 to $1500**







### **Best Time to Ship:**

### Fall & Spring (snowbirds)
